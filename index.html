DMC MealPass

A complete N-tier architecure capstone project wherein students uses their school ID as a means to pay for their purchases within the school cafeteria. With POS included, we used an RFID scanner for cashless payments. It also includes a web application wherein students can view their previous meal purchases and top-up history. This project is where we applied all that we have learned from 1st to 4th year college, applying all techniques and maintaining a clean code.

  • C#, Dapper, LinQ, ASP.NET Core MVC, API, SQL Server, DevExpress, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Capstone Project

Setup Connect

Small Enterprises Technology Upgrading Program (SETUP). An ongoing web application project assigned by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST). This is my first Laravel project, it allows small business owners to ask help from DOST to boost the efficiency of production of the businesses and can even request a capital for jumpstart their business. Additionally, users may collaborate with each other whenever a business requires help either via equipment or training personels.

  • PHP, Laravel, Vite, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL
